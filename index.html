A picture perfect day

Sandy toes and gentle breeze,
Seaside whispers, a sense of ease.
Blue skies stretch, vast and wide,
Footprints left by the changing tide.

Children's laughter, building sandcastles,
Waves that dance in playful hassles.
Sunshine's warmth on bronzed skin,
A peaceful day about to begin.

Seashells scattered along the shore,
Soft waves lapping, a tranquil encore.
Picnic blankets, laughter near,
Simple joys, the beach atmosphere.

by gwenno goode
